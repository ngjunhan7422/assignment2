OUR FACILITIES

Free Weight Area


The 1600 sq ft free weight area is able to house over 50 people and still able to maintain safe distancing

These are the equipments available at our gym currently
- Over 15 standard barbells
- Dumbbells ranging from 1kg to 100kg
- 5 squat racks and 5 smith machines
- Various modern machines to cater to the needs of different body parts


Cardio Area


The cardio machine area provides you with a calm and soothing environment to do you cardiovascular workout
These are the equipments available at our gym currently
- 10 standard treadmills
- 10 stationary bikes
- 5 rowers machine
- 5 stationary stair climber machine


Private Training Room


There are 10 private training room available which can hold up to 10 pax in the room.
Private rooms can be booked by gym members only and is subjected to first come first serve basis.
Training with our personal trainers has priority over booking of the rooms.


Toilets and Locker Room


There are over 100 lockers to safekeep your items while you train without worrying theft occurence.
There are also bathing and toilet facilities available which are maintained daily.
